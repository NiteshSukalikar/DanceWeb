Founder

Nrityalankar Mrs. Shraddha Bhide


An Outstanding Patron of Kathak Art Form Nrityalankar Mrs. Shraddha Bhide started her incredible journey of the Kathak art form at the tender age of 8 years under the able guidance and mentorship of Guru Sangeetacharya Mrs. Vaishali Dudhe. She has been a consistent and dedicated learner over the years. Her commitment to the art form is a benchmark for the fellow students.


    Her accreditations in Kathak are:
  • Alankar (M.A) in Kathak dancefrom Akhil Bhartiya Gandharva Mahavidyalaya (Dec, 2008)
  • Visharad (B.A) in Kathak dancefrom Akhil Bhartiya Gandharva Mahavidyalaya (April, 2004)
  • M.A in KathakM.A in Kathak dance from Kavikulaguru Kalidas University, Ramtek in 2019 with First Class

Her multi-talented personality reflects in her strong academic background as well. She holds a Masters in Management Studies (HR Specialisation) and a Bachelors in Science (Chemistry Specialisation).


“No person was ever honoured for what he received. Honour has been the reward for what he gave”


True to these words of wisdom, she took it on herself to share and spread the knowledge gained across years. She founded a Kathak Dance institute - “Siddhi Nrityakala Mandir” in the year 2006. Her aim to share the joy of learning the rich Kathak art form has gained steady momentum over the years. Currently her institute trains more than 300 students in Kathak Dance form. Under her able guidance, many students have undergone a complete transformation and have achieved hordes of success in Kathak art form.


In her untiring pursuit of achieving excellence in the art form, she has choreographed many famous dance programmes like:
  • “Nrityavilaya – A Kathak Fusion” - A unique experiment of converging diverse Dance forms like Korean, Belly, Sufi, Contemporary, Tap Dance with Kathak.
  • “Krishnarang” - Dance act depicting the different folds of dramatic life of Lord Krishna like “Putna Vadh”, “Govardhan Leela”, “Kalia Daman”, “Kansa Vadh”, “Draupadi Vastraharan”.
  • “Nisargarang” – Dance performances showcasing the forms of Mother Nature.
  • “Guru Archana” – A performance showing respect to Gods & Goddesses.
  • “Stree Gatha” – A dance representation on the various facets of a “Woman”.

Her solo performances in national as well as international forums have been appreciated by the connoisseurs of the art form. Mrs. Shraddha Bhide is also an honorary examiner for Akhil Bhartiya Gandharva Mahavidyalaya, Miraj. She has been a honorary judge for prestigious competitions organized by the Innerwheel Club, Rotary Club, Lions Club, Lokmat Sakhi Manch, SVKM Performing Arts Institute & Inter school and Inter College Competitions. With the blessings of Lord Natraja, she aspires to continue being a torch bearer, for spreading the joy of Kathak art form to all who value and cherish the same.
